About Me

Hello everyone,

First of all i would like to thank you for visiting my personal site, and i hope you enjoyed...

I am 24 years old, and a Gamer like most of you,having big passion for games. And one day i thought... Why not make my own games.
And so i went to youtube and got very interested in game development after seeing the whole process and found my first game engine-Game Maker Studio 2
Game Maker Studio 2 is a great game engine for starters. Since for the most part, you dont have to code at all! And you can just focus on other aspects,like design.
The only problem was, free version had limited amount of objects i could have in my game.. So i eventually left my project all frustrated and feeling down.

A year passed, and i wanted to give it all another try. But this time i gave Unity a chance. Especially since Unity is a well designed engine for making 2D games, which i had interest in designing.
And now i am still working on that project, bit by bit. At the moment it is called DREAMERS but i might change it eventually.
It has been very fun coding, designing and implementing everything to create something new, on my own.
I have planned few more 2D games, before i throw myself into trying to make something big, like a 3D.
