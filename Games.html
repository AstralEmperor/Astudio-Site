Zombie Shooter

This game has been made in Game Maker 2, as my first game.

I started from simple shapes,and after being pushed by my brother i improved graphics bit by bit.
Although not too professional, i am happy that i actually pushed myself past the simple shapes.And from then on i try to make things realistic looking,
even tho it isnt very optimal for a beginner indie. At least i am pushing myself to advance. And it pokes my eyes, when it doesnt look nice.

It was inspired by the game -Plants Vs Zombies
Since it was just a demo version of the program, it doesn't contain too much ,because of the limited number of game objects.

Gameplay consists of the player character named Mike,trying to defend against never ending hordes of zombies. There is a kill count at the top, which represents the number of killed zombies When zombies touch you, the player dies and leaves the Grave in place of death.

Dreamers

First game that i am profesionally making.It is made in Unity and everything is made by me except music.
I have it built as an application, and i am still working on it.
It has Main Menu and Pause Menu....Intro and 1 Main Scene since there is only 1 level at the moment.

Gameplay consists of a main character who finds himself stuck in the dream, and is unable to wake up normally.
He is traveling between dreams in hope of finding The Exit.
As he progresses he slowly starts remembering that the dream is his own creation, and thus he holds the power over it, and is able to alter.

Game is inspired by my best friend, who has an unusual fear of pigeons..
Main character is made to look like me, and Shikha NPC is made in likeness of my girlfriend.

Right now there isn't too much story and characters. But with time, it is gonna become a rich and interesting world I promise!
